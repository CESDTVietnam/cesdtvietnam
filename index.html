Welcome To CESDT Vietnam

The Centre of Excellence in Software Development & Training will have the state of the art training facility to offer & impart advance IT training program to aspirants from ASEAN & CLMV countries. The centre will serves as a core building block and a catalyst to enable widespread reach of high quality education and software development. This will help them to deliver IT education and to enable access to youngsters desirous of perusing carrier in Information Technology.
